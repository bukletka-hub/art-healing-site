### 1.3 Your Biggest Opportunity

**None of your Calgary competitors do content marketing well.** Canopy has no blog. Play Purpose Art has minimal SEO. Supporting Wellness has generic content. This means the entire first page of Google for "art healing Calgary", "creative wellness Calgary", and related keywords is wide open. Whoever builds consistent, quality content first will own this space.

### 1.4 Your Biggest Threat

Licensed art therapists (Play Purpose Art, Supporting Wellness, Healing Arts) have professional credentials that build instant trust. You need to differentiate clearly as **creative wellness** (not therapy) and build trust through content, testimonials, and visible results instead.

### 2.3 On-Page SEO Checklist (For Every Page/Post)

Every piece of content Claude publishes will follow this:

- [ ] Target keyword in H1 (only one H1 per page)
- [ ] Target keyword in URL slug
- [ ] Target keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Meta title: keyword + benefit + location (under 60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description: keyword + CTA (under 155 chars)
- [ ] At least 2 images with keyword-rich alt text
- [ ] Internal links to pillar page and 2-3 related cluster articles
- [ ] One external link to an authoritative source
- [ ] FAQ section with 3-5 questions (with FAQ schema markup)
- [ ] CTA to lead magnet or booking within the content
- [ ] Blog post minimum 1,000 words
- [ ] Heading hierarchy: H1 → H2 → H3 (never skip levels)
- [ ] Schema markup (Article for blog, LocalBusiness for service pages)
