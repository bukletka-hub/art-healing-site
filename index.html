Meet Vlady Zinkovska

Calgary-based artist offering creative wellness experiences, kids art education, and custom painting services. From expressive murals and canvas paintings to guided art healing workshops and children's drawing classes — every project is created with heart and attention to detail.

Available for commissions: murals, canvas paintings, graphite pencil sketches, portraits, and illustrations.

🌍 Multilingual: English, Russian, Ukrainian

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages are the kids art classes for?

Kids art classes are available for ages 4-14, grouped by developmental stage: Drawing Fundamentals (ages 4-7), Art Skills (ages 8-11), and Advanced Art (ages 12-14).

Do I need art experience for the workshops?

No experience needed. Art Healing workshops are designed for all skill levels and are focused on the creative process and wellness benefits rather than technical expertise.

What types of murals do you paint?

Murals include kids rooms, cafes, daycares, botanical designs, and modern wall art. Each mural is custom-designed to fit your space and vision.

How do I commission a painting?

Request a quote with details about size, style, and deadline. Prices start at $100 for illustrations, $350 for canvas paintings, and $480 for murals.

Are classes available in Russian or Ukrainian?

Yes, kids art classes are available in English, Russian, and Ukrainian.

Where are you located?

Based in Calgary, Alberta. We offer in-person classes and services throughout Calgary, and online workshops available Canada-wide.
