Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Do I need any art experience?
Absolutely not! Art healing is about the process, not the product. Our sessions are designed for complete beginners and experienced artists alike. All you need to bring is an open mind.
What happens in a typical session?
Each session begins with a short relaxation exercise, followed by guided creative activities using various art materials. We close with a gentle reflection. Sessions typically last 60–90 minutes.
Is this art therapy?
Art healing is creative wellness — it's about using art to feel better, reduce stress, and express yourself. It is not clinical therapy or a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you need clinical support, we encourage you to work with a licensed therapist.
What materials do I need for online courses?
Most exercises use basic supplies you probably already have — paper, colored pencils, markers, or paint. Each course includes a simple materials list, and we keep it affordable and accessible.
Where are in-person sessions held in Calgary?
We hold workshops at various creative spaces across Calgary. The exact location is shared with registered participants. We also offer private sessions at your chosen venue for groups and corporate events.
How much do sessions cost?
Pricing varies by session type. Group workshops start at an accessible rate, while 1-on-1 sessions are personalized to your needs. Contact us for current pricing and any available packages.
